The 17-Month Synergy Challenge

Predicting Soundscapes: An Experiment in Audiovisual Intuition.

This project documents a 17-month journey where I paired classic films from my watchlist with essential albums from my personal collection.

The goal was to test a creative hypothesis: based solely on a film's premise, could I predict its perfect musical counterpart?

Each entry includes a review of the film and a "Synergy Verdict"—analyzing whether my initial intuition about the album's aesthetic and "vibe" truly resonated with the film's narrative after watching it.

Project Overview

CONCEPT: This archive is the result of a 17-month challenge focused on exploring audiovisual synergy. The objective was to cross-reference classic cinematography with iconic discography, testing aesthetic intuition through a rigorous monthly curation process.

Throughout this period, each "Discovery" (film) was selected from a technical watchlist and paired with a "Reference" (album) that shared the same emotional or visual DNA. The process involved a critical analysis of tone, rhythm, and atmosphere to validate the compatibility between both works.

TECHNICAL SPECS: Developed using a "Vanilla Stack" (HTML5, CSS3, and pure JavaScript), focusing on performance, responsive design, and an immersive navigation experience.

ACKNOWLEDGMENTS: To all the artists, directors, and musicians whose works served as the foundation for this curation and design case study.
